About

Hewang Nie is a Lecturer with a Ph.D. in Cyberspace Security from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (June 2025). His research focuses on AI security, reversible information hiding, and neural network watermarking.

He has led a student innovation funding project from the Cyberspace Administration of China and participated in major research programs including the National Key R&D Program of China and the National Natural Science Foundation of China. He has published over ten papers in well-known journals (IEEE TAI, IPM, KBS, ESWA) and conferences (ICASSP, Euro-Par), holding 3 invention patents.

Research Interests

  • Multimedia Security; Neural Network Watermarking; Reversible Data Hiding
