About

Hewang Nie is a Lecturer with a Ph.D. in Cyberspace Security from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (June 2025). His research focuses on AI security, reversible information hiding, and neural network watermarking.

He has led a student innovation funding project from the Cyberspace Administration of China and participated in major research programs including the National Key R&D Program of China and the National Natural Science Foundation of China. He has published over ten papers in well-known journals and conferences, including five papers in SCI Q1 (TOP) journals and six papers in CCF-B journals/conferences.

Service

  • Member of the Digital Media Forensics and Security Committee, CSIG.
  • Program Committee Member for Workshop on New-Generation Image Security Technologies.
  • Journal Reviewer: IEEE TAI, IEEE Access, KBS, ESWA, EAAI, IPM, Information Fusion, Neural Networks, etc.
  • Conference Reviewer: ICASSP, ICME, IJCNN, etc.
