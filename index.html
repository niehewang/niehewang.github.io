个人简介

聂何望，男，汉族，籍贯江西省九江市，博士/讲师。2025年6月毕业于华中科技大学，获网络与信息安全博士学位。长期致力于人工智能安全、可逆信息隐藏、神经网络模型水印等领域的研究。

About

Hewang Nie is a Lecturer with a Ph.D. in Cyberspace Security from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (June 2025). His research focuses on AI security, reversible information hiding, and neural network watermarking.

He has led a student innovation funding project from the Cyberspace Administration of China and participated in major research programs including the National Key R&D Program of China and the National Natural Science Foundation of China. He has published over ten papers in well-known journals and conferences, including six papers in SCI Q1 (TOP) journals and eight papers in CCF-B journals/conferences. He holds 3 authorized invention patents and 3 software copyrights.
